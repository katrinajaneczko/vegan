So, Why Go Vegan?

  • For the planet:
    • Eating a plant-based diet means lessening your personal negative environmental impact. For example, it decreases your carbon and water footprints and less land is required to support your diet since you're eating lower on the food chain.
  • For the animals:
    • It reduces the unnecessary suffering of animals used for their meat and byproducts. Unfortunately, farm animals are often kept in atrocious conditions, abused, exploited, and killed inhumanely.
  • For your health:
    • Scientific studies show a connection between a balanced vegan diet and important health benefits. A healthy vegan diet can reduce cholesterol, control hypertension, and protect against cancer, osteoporosis, and heart disease.
